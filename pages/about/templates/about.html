About us

Hokus Mokus, also known as Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes, was a joke shop located at Number 93 Diagon Alley founded by Fred and George Weasley.
The shop contained practical joke objects. This establishment started out as an 'owl-post service' led by the twins from the Burrow,
and later continued from Hogwarts, selling joke-products until the store front was opened.
The shop was the Weasley twins' dream, they got the seed money for the shop from Harry Potter

Fred participated in the Battle of Hogwarts (1998), defending the castle's passageways with his twin. While fighting he was killed in an explosion.

Today George and his younger brother Ron manged together the company and continue to bring laugh and joy to young wizards and muggle.
